=== title: Metastable Dance created: 2011-02-23 00:00:00 published: True description: > A string quartet with shifting time signatures that I composed in 2005. === *Metastable Dance* is a modern string quartet that I composed in 2005. The time signature shifts throughout the piece but settles into 5/4 for the main theme. It was performed by a string quartet that was visiting MIT, who prefer to remain anonymous because of the fact that they didn't rehearse much for this recording.
